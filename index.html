About Us

We are located (location) right on the river. We are surronded by orchids and have stunning dock on the river. Our Start

We started by taking an average dive bar and creating a great dive bar. With the addition of a dock, new furniture, a groovy playlist, a diverse drinks menu, and lots of smiles and laughs. We've created a hidden gem, under the bridge. We have always acknowldged our humble beginnings and want to stay true to them. We are here to talk, laugh, and stumble with you.
